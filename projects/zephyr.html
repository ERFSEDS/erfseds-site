ZEPHYR

ABout Zephyr

The primary focus of Zephyr is the design of a supersonic capable recovery system and an airframe to house it. The applications of our research range from atmospheric re-entry to the testing of electronics and parachute designs under high acceleration. Being in Project Zephyr leads members in out of the box thinking as well as unique research and design.

The project is looking for people interested in recovery, electronics, and manufacturing. However, everyone who wants to join is welcome .
