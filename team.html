Officers



President

Antonia Tompkins
Antonia Tompkins is a senior in Aerospace Engineering with a concentration in Astronautics who is excited to be President again to work with an incredible family of space enthusiasts. She has been an active member all four years, starting with ERFSEDS her freshmen year on Pathfinder VII. In the following year, she lead the Pathfinder VIII team as a sophomore. As a junior Antonia became the President of ERFSEDS, then took a year to work on projects and take an internship as a senior. Antonia's goal is to make rocketry accessible to anyone and everyone with a passion or interest to contribute to ERFSEDS's mission. ERFSEDS has given her multiple opportunities and learning experiences that has set the foundation for her engineering career which she hope to help share with others.

Project Leads



Icarus Lead

Justin Nauman
Justin Nauman is a senior in Aerospace engineering with a concentration in astronautics. He is currently the Project Icarus Lead for ERFSEDS. Justin has been heavily involved in ERFSEDS since his freshman year. Prior to becoming Icarus Lead, Justin served as Chief Engineer for one semester before taking a hiatus from school to work as a platform systems engineer at Collins Aerospace (Raytheon Technologies), working on the Future Attack and Reconnaissance Aircraft (FARA). Aside from Chief Engineer was an active member of Project Hummingbird where he was a thread lead for the nose cone team. Along with ERFSEDS, Justin is part of the ERAU track and cross country teams and a member of the Fellowship of Christian Athletes (FCA). He enjoys building his own RC airplanes and high power rockets and is currently level 3 high power certified through the National Association of Rocketry (NAR).
